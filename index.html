Welcome to Pal Hotel, your serene retreat in the heart of Ladakh. Experience unparalleled comfort and breathtaking views, where modern amenities blend seamlessly with the region's rich cultural heritage. Perfect for travelers seeking tranquility and adventure.

Ask Your Queries

Guided Trekking Tours

Explore Ladakh's majestic landscapes with our expert-guided trekking tours. From scenic day hikes to challenging mountain trails, our tours are designed for all levels, providing you with unforgettable adventures and breathtaking views of the region's natural beauty.

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in Ladakh's rich cultural heritage with our curated cultural experiences. Visit ancient monasteries, witness traditional dance performances, and learn about local customs and crafts. These experiences offer a deeper understanding of the region's unique history and traditions.

Spa and Wellness

Rejuvenate your mind and body at our luxurious spa and wellness center. Offering a range of treatments including traditional Ladakhi therapies, massages, and yoga sessions, our wellness services ensure complete relaxation and revitalization during your stay.

Gourmet Dining

Savor the flavors of Ladakh at our gourmet dining restaurant. Our chefs prepare a variety of local and international dishes using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Enjoy a delightful culinary experience in a serene and elegant setting.
